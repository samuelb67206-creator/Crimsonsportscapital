About Us

Investing Across the Sports Ecosystem

Crimson Sports Capital is the dedicated sports investment division of a multi-generational family office. We deploy patient, strategic capital across the sports ecosystem — from team ownership to emerging technologies, youth sports, college athletics, and diversified real estate.

Our approach combines deep industry relationships with long-term conviction, partnering with operators and founders who are shaping the future of sports.
